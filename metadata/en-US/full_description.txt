AKN Music is a simple, elegant and open source music player for Android.

Features:
- Clean and modern user interface
- Background playback with notification controls
- Playlist management
- Repeat one/all functionality
- Theme customization
- No ads, no tracking, completely free and open source

This application respects your privacy and doesn't require any unnecessary permissions.
